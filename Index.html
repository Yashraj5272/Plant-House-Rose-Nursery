About Us

Our Story

Plant House Rose Nursery was born from a simple love of nature and a dream to share that passion with our community. What started as a small backyard greenhouse has blossomed into a thriving nursery dedicated to exceptional plants and unparalleled service.

We believe in the power of plants to transform spaces, improve well-being, and connect us to the natural world.

  • Quality — We never compromise on plant health and vitality
  • Sustainability — Eco-friendly practices at the heart of all we do
  • Customer Care — Your success is our success
  • Passion — It shows in every plant we grow
